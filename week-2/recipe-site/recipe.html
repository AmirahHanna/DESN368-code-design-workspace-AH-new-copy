Simple Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tablespoon olive oil, butter, or bacon grease

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs, salt, and pepper together until lightly frothy.
  2. In an 8-inch non-stick pan, heat the oil over medium-low heat. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to make sure they're evenly spread out, then turn the heat down to low.
    Cook the omelette for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the edges are cooked through and the top is slightly wet but not runny.
  3. If you'd like to add filling, add it to half of the omelette. Then use a spatula to fold the empty half on top and slide the omelette onto a plate. Garnish with chopped herbs.

About This Recipe

An omlette is a simple, straightforward meal that you can make with almost any customizations. It's cheap and easy for an everyday breakfast, not counting eggflation!
